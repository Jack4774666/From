The Town

The place does not want them to leave.

FROM follows the residents of a mysterious town that traps anyone unlucky enough to enter. The survivors try to preserve a normal life, but each night the surrounding forest releases creatures that look human, speak softly, and kill brutally. The show mixes survival drama, supernatural horror, family trauma, mythology, and a constantly expanding puzzle.

Season 1 · 2022

The Arrival

The Matthews family enters the town, Boyd tries to keep order, and the audience learns the brutal rules of nightfall.

  • Introduces the road loop.
  • Establishes Colony House and Township.
  • Reveals the talisman defense.
  • Ends with deeper questions underground.
Season 2 · 2023

The Bus

A bus full of new survivors arrives, resources are stretched, and the town becomes more aggressive.

  • New arrivals reshape the social balance.
  • Boyd brings back a terrifying infection.
  • Dreams, visions, and voices intensify.
  • The mystery expands beyond the streets.
Season 3 · 2024

The Pattern

The show pushes harder into trauma, sacrifice, reincarnation, and the hidden design behind the town.

  • Tabitha’s journey changes the escape question.
  • Jade’s visions become more important.
  • Victor’s memories gain new weight.
  • The Man in Yellow becomes unavoidable.
Season 4 · 2026

The Cost

The closer the residents get to answers, the more the town punishes them for asking.

  • More focus on the town’s origin.
  • Higher cost for every discovery.
  • Man in Yellow mythology deepens.
  • Leads toward the final season.
Rules & Mythology

Survival has rules. The town has worse ones.

These cards are written like premium streaming-site lore blocks. They give the viewer the feeling of the show without overexplaining everything.

🌲

The Forest

The forest surrounds the town like a living boundary. It hides monsters, impossible trees, visions, voices, and paths that should not exist.

🪨

The Talismans

Strange carved stones protect enclosed spaces from the creatures. They do not explain the town, but they give the residents one thing more precious than truth: a night of survival.

🛣️

The Looping Road

Leave by car and the road returns you to town. The trap does not behave like geography. It behaves like a decision already made.

🧴

The Bottle Trees

Bottles hanging from branches form one of the town’s most haunting symbols. Their purpose connects memory, ritual, movement, and the deeper mythology.

👦

The Children

Pale children appear in visions and symbols. They are tied to the oldest horror beneath the town, and saving them may be connected to freeing everyone.

🟡

The Man in Yellow

A sinister figure connected to the town’s deeper design. He represents knowledge, manipulation, and the terrifying possibility that the nightmare has an intelligence.
